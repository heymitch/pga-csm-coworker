# Call Briefs Playbook

## Pre-Call Brief Template

```
## 1:1 Call Brief: [Student Name]
**Call Type:** [Onboarding / Outreach Strategy / Sales Review / Delight & Upsell / Wildcard]
**Date/Time:** [from Calendly]

### Student Status
- **Enrollment:** [Deposit $X / Fully Enrolled]
- **Agreement:** [Signed / Unsigned — ESCALATE IF UNSIGNED]
- **Week in Program:** [X of 16]
- **Current Module:** [X]
- **Last Assignment:** [Module X, submitted DATE]
- **Days Since Last Activity:** [X]

### Recent Activity
- [Latest Slack messages/questions]
- [Latest assignment submission summary]
- [Any flags: inactive, stuck, frustrated]

### Call Agenda
[Call-type-specific agenda — see below]

### Talking Points
- [Specific to this student's situation]
- [Reference their actual work/submissions]
```

## Call-Type Agendas

### Call 1: Onboarding (30 min)
**Trigger:** After enrollment + agreement signed. Within first 24-72 hours ideally.
**NOTE:** Student can leave program within 72 hours — get them on this call ASAP.

1. **Intro + Rapport** (3 min) — Name, welcome, where from, connection
2. **Housekeeping** (3 min) — Agreement signed? Slack + Skool access? Meet & Greet scheduled?
3. **Goals** (5 min) — What brought you to PGA? Non-monetary goals? Monetary goals?
   - Key line: "Our focus is getting to that first paying client. If we do it once, we can do it 100 times."
4. **Program Walkthrough** (5 min) — Slack for questions, Skool for curriculum, 1:1 calls per milestone
5. **Custom Success Path** (5 min) — Living breathing course guide, checkboxes, walk through each call trigger
6. **Support & Accountability** (5 min) — Slack (no DMs!), Skool search, live clinics, Accountability Trios
7. **Next Steps** (4 min) — Meet & Greet, next assignment + target date

**4-Week Action Plan:**
- Module 0: within 72 hours of enrollment
- Module 1: within 1 week
- Module 2: within 2 weeks
- Module 3: within 3 weeks
- Module 4: within 4 weeks (EEC launched!)

### Call 2: Outreach Strategy (20 min)
**Trigger:** After Assignment 5 submitted (Contact Dashboard + Leaks/Faucets in #submit-assignment-5)

1. **Rapport** (2 min) — Congrats on launching EEC + creating dashboard
2. **Contact Dashboard Review** (8 min) — Screen share, review completeness, leaks vs. faucets balance, started outreach?
3. **Outreach Message** (5 min) — Review messages sent or send one LIVE on the call
4. **Outreach Schedule** (5 min) — Share Daily/Weekly Schedule, talk through their ideal cadence for 25/week

**Key lines:**
- "Outreach is the lifeblood of your ghostwriting business"
- "If people fall off, this is where they fall off"
- "What would it take to land a paid client this month?"

### Call 3: Sales Call Review (20 min)
**Trigger:** After Assignment 7 submitted

1. Watch recording before call (or review self-reflection if no recording)
2. Identify 1 key improvement area — don't critique everything
3. Discuss follow-up plan for this prospect
4. Check ongoing outreach — not all eggs in one basket
5. Next steps toward Module 8

### Call 4: Delight & Upsell (20 min)
**Trigger:** After Assignment 8 submitted
- Celebrate client landing
- Review fulfillment experience
- Discuss recurring revenue path
- Introduce Liftoff if appropriate ($5K clients landed threshold)

### Call 5: Wildcard (20 min)
**Trigger:** As needed — pre-sales call, outreach bottleneck, stuck point
- Student-driven agenda
- Focus on their specific blocker

### Call 6: Business Strategy / Offboarding (30 min)
**Trigger:** Week 16 or $5K clients landed (Liftoff eligible)
- See offboarding-students playbook for full script

## Post-Call Follow-Up Template

```
Hey @[Student]! Great call today.

**Recording:** [Fathom/Zoom link]

**Key Takeaways:**
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]

**Your Next Steps:**
1. [Action item 1 — with deadline]
2. [Action item 2 — with deadline]
3. [Action item 3 — with deadline]

Can you confirm these next steps so we're aligned? Let's go!
```

Post in student's **private channel** (not DM).
Ask them to **confirm their next steps** in the channel.
